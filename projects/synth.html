Analog Audio Synthesizer

Skills: Soldering, Circuit Debugging, Woodworking, Web Development, Collaboration

About this project: For Build18 2025, CMU's annual ECE Hardware Hackathon, my friend and I are building an analog audio synthesizer and developing a web interface to record the output. We chose to build our synth because of interests in signal processing and music production.

The synthesizer features 1/4in jack input and output, compatible with a standard microphone or electric guitar.
