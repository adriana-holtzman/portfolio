Analog Audio Synthesizer

Skills: Soldering, Circuit Debugging, Woodworking, Collaboration, Public Speaking

About this project: For Build18 2025, CMU's annual ECE Hardware Hackathon, my friend and I chose to build an analog audio synthesizer and work towards developing a web interface to record the output. We chose to build our synth because of interests in signal processing and music production.

The synthesizer features:

  • 1/4in jack input, compatible with standard microphone, electric guitar, and more
  • 1/4in jack output, compatible with most speakers or amps
  • Filtering, echo, low frequency oscillator, and high frequency oscillator functionality, such that the synth can manipulate input signals and create its own

This project was selected for an Innov18 presentation to a collection of Build18 corporate sponsors. Here is our presentation: SuperSynth.
