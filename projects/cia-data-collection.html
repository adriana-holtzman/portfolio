What is CIA Buggy? Carnegie Involvement Association, familiarly known as CIA Buggy, is a student organization at CMU. We participate in one of CMU's annual traditions: racing our buggies (carbon fiber gravity racers) against 10+ teams during Raceday, with the help of our mechanics, drivers, pushers, and build team. For Raceday 2025, I am leading CIA's data collection and analysis efforts as Data Chair.
